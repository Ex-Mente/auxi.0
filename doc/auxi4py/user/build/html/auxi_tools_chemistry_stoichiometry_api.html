auxi.tools.chemistry.stoichiometry.convert_compound((float)mass, (str)source, (str)target, (str)element) → float :¶

Convert the specified mass of the source compound to the target using element as basis.

Parameters:
  • mass – Mass of from_compound. [kg]
  • source – Formula and phase of the original compound, e.g. “Fe2O3[S1]”.
  • target – Formula and phase of the target compound, e.g. “Fe[S1]”.
  • element – Element to use as basis for the conversion, e.g. “Fe” or “O”.
Returns:

Mass of to_compound. [kg]

auxi.tools.chemistry.stoichiometry.molar_mass([(str)compound]) → float :¶

Determine the molar mass of a chemical compound. The molar mass is usually the mass of one mole of the substance, but here it is the mass of 1000 moles, since the mass unit used in pmpy is kg.

Parameters:compound – Formula of a chemical compound, e.g. “Fe2O3”.
Returns:Molar mass. [kg/kmol]
